Our Vision

Fast-tracking the
most able young talent

The Accelerator is built for players identified as more able and talented. It takes them, at the age development matters most, and accelerates their growth with coaching, standards and an environment club rugby cannot offer.

This is not club rugby.
Squad members are prospects. A place is earned by assessment, held by performance, and open to a select few.

What We Stand For

Built to accelerate talent
development, not just play

01

More able and talented

Built specifically for players identified as more able and talented, to push development well beyond the club game.

02

Strength & performance

Age-appropriate conditioning built on long-term athletic development, so prospects are ready when representative rugby calls.

03

Skills & game sense

High-quality coaching that sharpens core skills, decision-making and game intelligence at the age it sticks.

04

Individual focus

Small squads and high coach-to-player ratios. Every prospect developed as an individual, with feedback that moves the needle.

05

Local Talent ID

We identify local talent early and accelerate it fast, benchmarking progress against the prospects who graduate the programme. We keep tabs on future Pooler players, and link with Regional Age Grade teams for the step up.
