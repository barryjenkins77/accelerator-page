PRIVACY POLICY Pontypool RFC Accelerator Programme Last updated: July 2026 1. WHO WE ARE GARETH BETTS STRENGTH & PERFORMANCE LTD (Company number 17104473) operates the Accelerator, an elite youth rugby development programme for players aged 11 to 14. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use and protect personal data. 2. WHAT DATA WE COLLECT When you register a player for the Accelerator, we collect: - Player's full name - Player's date of birth - Current school year - Current school - Current rugby club (if any) - Playing position - Parent or guardian name - Parent or guardian email address - Parent or guardian phone number - Any additional information you provide about the player 3. HOW WE USE YOUR DATA We use the data you provide to: - Assess whether a player meets the programme benchmarks - Invite selected players to attend assessment and training - Contact parents about programme updates, fees and scheduling - Manage the player's participation in the Accelerator - Keep emergency contact records We do not use your data for marketing, profiling, or sharing with third parties without your explicit consent. 4. LEGAL BASIS We collect and process this data under GDPR Article 6(1)(a) - your consent when you submit the registration form. Parents and guardians must consent to data collection before submission. 5. HOW LONG WE KEEP YOUR DATA We keep registration data for the duration of the player's time in the Accelerator plus two years after they leave the programme, to maintain records of participation and progression. After that period, we delete the data securely. If a player is not selected for the Accelerator following assessment, we keep the data for one year before deletion. 6. PHOTOGRAPHS AND VIDEO During Accelerator training and events, we may take photographs or video of players for: - Internal coaching and performance analysis - Club records and player development tracking - Social media and club communications (with explicit consent only) - Occasional media coverage of matches or events Important: You control this. When you register, you will be asked to give separate consent for: a) Photography and video for coaching and internal club use b) Photography and video for social media and public club communications You can consent to one, both, or neither. If you consent to coaching use only, we will not publish images or video publicly. 7. OPT OUT AT ANY TIME You can opt out of any data use at any time, including photography and video, by contacting the Accelerator Coaching Team. We will stop collecting new images and delete any unpublished material within 7 days. If you opt out of social media use but previously consented, we will remove published images and video from social channels within 14 days where technically possible. We cannot remove images already printed in hard-copy materials. 8. YOUR RIGHTS UNDER GDPR You have the right to: - Access the data we hold about you (request a copy at any time) - Correct data that is inaccurate - Delete your data (subject to our retention policy above) - Withdraw consent at any time - Complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) if you believe we have mishandled your data To exercise any of these rights, contact the Accelerator Coaching Team with your request. 9. DATA SECURITY We store all data securely using Google Sheets with password protection. We do not store data on personal devices or unencrypted email. Only authorised coaching and admin staff have access to registration data. In the event of a data breach, we will notify all affected parents within 72 hours. 10. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify parents of any material changes by email. 11.
